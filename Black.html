Black Soil

Rich in Nutrients for Cotton and Other Crops

Soil Characteristics

Black soil, also known as Regur soil, is ideal for growing cotton, as it retains moisture well. It is rich in calcium, magnesium, and potassium, essential for agricultural growth.

Wheat

Jowar

Millets
