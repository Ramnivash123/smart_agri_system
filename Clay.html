Clay Soil

Dense and Moisture-Retaining Soil for Water-Loving Crops

Soil Characteristics

Clay soil has small, tightly packed particles, which allows it to retain moisture well. It's excellent for growing crops that thrive in heavy, moist soil.

Lettuce

Chard

Broccoli
